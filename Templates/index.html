Summary: America's Opioid Pandemic

In this project, we use machine learning models such as Multi-Linear Regressiona and Times Series ARIMA to predict future Opioid prescription claim amounts. The historical data gathered included included opioid claim amounts from 2006 - 2017 in the United States. The Opioid Epedemic began in the early 2000s as opioids were initially prescribed by doctors for physical pain management. As the prescription claim amount increased, research began to discovery the reason for addiction and manage usage.

Why a Mulitple Linear Regression?

At first, we analyzed our data with a multiple linear regression model was to get a feel for the overall opioid trending for each state. This model shows very basic linear trending data from one year to the next.
